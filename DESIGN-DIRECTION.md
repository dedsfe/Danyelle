# Danyelle Vieira — Design Direction

## Subject, audience, and job

- Subject: Danyelle Vieira's mobile-first event capture and editing service.
- Audience: people and brands choosing someone to record an event and turn it into social-ready video.
- Single job: make the visitor feel the quality of her coverage and start a booking conversation.

## Settled direction

- Use Creatoroly as the primary structural and motion reference: minimal interface, generous white space, confident type, constant proof through moving work.
- Keep the interface silent. Videos are the visual emphasis, the proof, and the product.
- Avoid generic AI-site aesthetics, gratuitous glass, decorative cards, dark-and-neon SaaS styling, and conventional “premium” cues.
- Start with a white splash. Reveal “Danyelle Vieira” from left to right, let it dissolve through blur, then reveal the header.
- Use selected services and short copy rather than explaining everything.
- Treat recording by phone as proximity, spontaneity, discretion, social-native framing, and speed—not as a limitation.

## Signature element

Videos begin as scattered fragments and become navigable portals. During scroll they move through depth, grow to dominate the viewport, change between landscape and 9:16, and let the visitor visually enter an event. Vertical scroll may drive horizontal media movement when it strengthens the sequence.

## Motion rules

- Orchestrate motion around the media; do not animate every UI element.
- Use scroll transformations to preserve spatial continuity between thumbnail, featured video, and event detail.
- Use critically damped springs by default and bounce only after momentum-driven gestures.
- Keep transformations interruptible when users can directly manipulate them.
- Provide a reduced-motion path using short cross-fades and static layouts.
- Inspect intermediate frames for clipped type, clipped blur, strobing, and layout jumps.

## Media and mockups

- Real Danyelle footage carries the page. Stock imagery is only atmospheric support.
- Mockups contextualize deliverables but never replace proof.
- Optimize video with posters, lazy loading, responsive sources, muted inline playback, and progressive loading. Visual quality may feel 4K without downloading every master at page load.

## Apple influence

- Borrow restraint, spatial consistency, optical typography, native-feeling response, and purposeful motion.
- Apple-style emoji must render as image assets so Windows cannot substitute Segoe UI Emoji.
- Exact Apple emoji artwork has licensing ambiguity; keep this a conscious project decision.

## Current scope

Only the splash and provisional header exist. Do not add hero or page sections until explicitly requested.
